AI GYM TRAINER

The AI Gym Trainer project is a cutting-edge solution designed to
revolutionize the gym and fitness industry by harnessing the power of
artificial intelligence and computer vision. Its primary goal is to provide
gym-goers with a personalized and interactive experience, offering them
guidance, motivation, and real-time feedback during their workouts.
It is a virtual fitness assistant that utilizes artificial intelligence
to provide personalized workout guidance, track progress,and offer feedback.
It can analyze user data, such as fitness levels and goals,to create tailored
exercise routines, adapt workouts based on performance, and offer suggestions for
improvement. This technology aims to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of
individualized fitness training by leveraging AI algorithms.
